=== Intro Screen ===
IMPORTANT! PLEASE READ!
To navigate through the game, you will mostly only need to press the ENTER-Key.
However, there will be times when you will be asked to make a choice.
In those cases, you will be given a list of options to choose from.
You can select an option by typing the corresponding key and pressing ENTER.
Please wait for the lines to finish printing before pressing ENTER
There's no way to jump lines or skip text...
If you press ENTER too early, the game will not work as intended.
Thank you for your understanding! And happy gaming!

Press ENTER to continue.
